=== MAGCAST ===

Magcast is a Simple, Clean and Beautiful Responsive Magazine WordPress Theme. It is easily customizable with customize. Some of the highlighted options are two site layout (Narrow and Wide), 4 layouts for every post/page, 2 Page Templates, 8 Widget Areas, 5 Custom Widgets, Featured slider to highlight your post, Social Profile Navigation and many more. Also has custom background, header, menu, favicon, CSS, webclip icon, and logo. Supports popular plugins like Breadcrumb NavXT, WP-PageNavi and Contact Form 7. It is also translation ready. Get free support at http://themehorse.com/support-forum/ and View beautiful Magazine demo site at http://themehorse.com/preview/magcast
